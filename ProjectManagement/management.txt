Trials and errors:
    - Ghost movements:
        We encountered lots and lots of errors when trying to do the ghost move.
        Since we are moving the players and ghosts based on their speed and the fps, sometimes
        ghosts were simply not moving or stucking themselves onto walls.
    - Maze:
        When it was time to put the level onto the screen, we had issues and found out the method we were
        using was nowhere near the right way so **erifflar** ended up redoing the entire thing.
        An example of what was wrong is that sometimes walls were overlapping and sometimes not in the
        right place.
    - Respawn mechanic:
        We wanted a respawn as close as the pacman one. Which mean when you die, everything is set back to
        their spawn and, after a countdown of 3 seconds, the game goes on.
        The problem **nisalmon** had was when dying, the game froze directly, and after the countdown, unfroze
        and respawn everything.


Finished project:
    For this project we really wanted to stay as close as possible to the original Pacman but soon enough,
    we found out that it wouldn't be as easy as we could've thought.
    So we tried our best and it turned out pretty great, our project is not completely like the original Pacman
    but we are proud of it and that's what matters.
